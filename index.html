Frequently Asked Questions

How long does SAM.gov registration take?

SAM.gov registration typically takes 7-10 business days once all required documentation is submitted. We ensure your application is complete and accurate to avoid delays and rejections.

What's included in your LLC formation service?

Our LLC formation service includes name availability check, articles of organization filing, operating agreement drafting, EIN application, registered agent service, and guidance on ongoing compliance requirements.

Do you provide construction services in addition to business consulting?

Yes! King David Contracting LLC offers both business consulting services and professional construction services. Visit our construction division at kingdavidcontracting.com for more details about our building and renovation services.

What makes your approach different?

We provide "Custom Solutions" - meaning we tailor our services to your specific needs rather than offering one-size-fits-all packages. Whether you need business formation, government contracting setup, or construction services, we adapt our approach to fit your unique situation.

What are your service areas?

We're based in Vineland, NJ and primarily serve South Jersey and the greater Philadelphia region. However, many of our business consulting services (like SAM.gov setup and LLC formation) can be provided remotely to clients nationwide.
